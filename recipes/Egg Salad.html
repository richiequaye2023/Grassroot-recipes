Egg Salad

Description of Egg Salad

This egg salad tastes wonderful in a sandwich and will definitely be devoured! It's really good on rye. Make deli-worthy egg salad sandwiches at home with this top-rated egg salad recipe! You'll come back to it again and again.

Ingredients:

  • 8 eggs
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup chopped green onion
  • ¼ cup chopped green onion
  • ¼ cup chopped green onion
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps or Direction:

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat.
  2. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.
  3. Remove from hot water, cool, peel, and chop.
  4. Place chopped eggs in a bowl; stir in mayonnaise, green onion, and mustard.
  5. Season with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  6. Stir and serve on your favorite bread or crackers.
